Resource Library

Welcome to the Resource Library! Explore a collection of recipe books and resources from Palestine and around the world. Discover diverse cuisines and their cultural heritage.

Recipe Books and Resources from Palestine

  • The Palestinian Table by Reem Kassis - A beautiful cookbook blending traditional Palestinian recipes with modern touches.
  • Taste of Palestine - A non-profit website offering authentic recipes and cultural insights.
  • Academia: Palestinian Food Studies - Access scholarly articles and free resources on Palestinian cuisine and culture.
  • NPR: The Palestinian Table Review - A detailed review of Palestinian recipes and their cultural significance.

Global Recipe Books and Resources

  • AllRecipes - A massive collection of global recipes contributed by a community of food lovers.
  • Epicurious - Offers recipes from various cuisines, including traditional and modern dishes.
  • TasteAtlas - An interactive map of global dishes, with detailed descriptions and recipes.
  • BBC Good Food - International recipes and cooking tips for both beginners and experienced cooks.
  • Project Gutenberg: Historical Cookbooks - Access digitized versions of classic cookbooks for free.

Free E-Books and Articles

  • Internet Archive - Search for free e-books and recipes, including books on Palestinian and international cuisine.
  • Open Culture Free E-Books - A collection of free e-books, including food and culinary topics.
  • Smashwords - A selection of free and paid e-books, with many on global cooking and cultural recipes.
